About Us

Welcome to GroceWave, your premier destination for quality groceries and exceptional service. With three convenient branches located in Colombo, Kandy, and Gampola. At GroceWave, we're committed to providing you with the freshest produce, Dairy products, and cosmetic products, all under one roof. Our diverse selection caters to every palate and preference, ensuring that you find exactly what you need for every meal and occasion. But GroceWave is more than just a grocery store - we're a vital part of the communities we serve. Our friendly staff at each branch are dedicated to offering personalized assistance and expert guidance to make your shopping experience a breeze. Whether you're stocking up for the week or seeking inspiration for your next culinary adventure, GroceWave is here to exceed your expectations. Visit one of our branches today and discover the GroceWave difference firsthand.
