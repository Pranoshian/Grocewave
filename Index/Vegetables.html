Vegetables & Fruits

Three venues where vegetables & Fruits are provided for our supermarket.

Veggie Heaven Market

Step into a paradise for vegetable enthusiasts at Veggie Haven Market, an expansive venue within the Gorce Wave Supermarket dedicated exclusively to plant-based delights. Here, vegetables reign supreme, showcased in all their colorful glory against a backdrop of lush greenery. From heirloom tomatoes to vibrant bell peppers, the selection is as diverse as it is enticing, catering to vegans, vegetarians, and omnivores alike.

Read more

Green Grocer's Guild

Explore a treasure trove of fresh produce at the Green Grocer's Guild, a bustling marketplace within the Gorce Wave Supermarket dedicated to promoting sustainable agriculture and supporting local farmers. From leafy greens to seasonal specialties, the Guild offers a wide variety of vegetables sourced from nearby farms and cooperatives.

Read more

Vegetable Villa

Step into a world of culinary inspiration at Vegetable Villa, an innovative venue within the Gorce Wave Supermarket that caters to aspiring chefs and home cooks alike. Here, vegetables take center stage, showcased in a variety of creative displays and themed sections designed to spark culinary creativity.
